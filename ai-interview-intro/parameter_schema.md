# Introduction Parameters

The system evaluates introductions based on **20 parameters**.

Each detected parameter = **1 mark**

Total score = **20**

---

1. Greeting
2. Name
3. Age
4. Course / Degree
5. Year of study
6. College / School
7. Location
8. Area of interest
9. Skills
10. Hobbies
11. Strengths
12. Achievements
13. Projects
14. Internship experience
15. Career goal
16. Passion or motivation
17. Teamwork ability
18. Leadership qualities
19. Learning mindset
20. Closing statement
